Unit 7 Ideals Prewrite

  • Due Friday by 11:59pm
  • Points 12
  • Submitting an external tool

Today's Assignment: 

  • Pick three ideas for each "side" and write about them for their own assessment.
    • Students should try to choose three topics that could go together in a full paragraph eventually (in the end of the year Culminating Ideals Essay). 
    • Students should write as many details as possible about each piece of evidence now while the information is fresh in your mind. 
    • You can bullet the information. It does not have to be in paragraph form at this time.  
    • BE SURE TO EXPLAIN WHY YOU CHOOSE THE IDEAL YOU CHOSE OR YOU WILL NOT GET FULL CREDIT

A few "HINTS" to writing the Pre-write for this Unit: Look at your Vocabulary list, Postwar Adjustment and Postwar Adjustment Wrapup (-), Postwar Boom (+/-), Presidents (+), Traditionalism/Moderism (+/-) 1920s Popular Culture Questions (+/-), Study Guide (+/-)

ALSO: Do NOT use things happening in Europe as evidence we are only examining AMERICAN IDEALS
